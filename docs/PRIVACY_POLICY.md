# LessDo Privacy Policy

Effective June 13, 2026.

LessDo stores lists, tasks, settings, and focus history locally on the user's
device. Version 1.0 does not require an account and does not operate an
analytics or advertising backend.

Notification permission is used only for reminders created by the user.
Biometric authentication is performed by the operating system; LessDo does not
receive or store biometric data. Optional purchases are processed by the
platform store. List content is shared only when the user invokes the system
Share command.

The publisher must add a support email before publishing this policy.
